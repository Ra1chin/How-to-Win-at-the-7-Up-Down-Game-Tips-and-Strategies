How to Play the 7 Up Down Game

Playing the 7 Up Down Game is straightforward. Here’s a quick breakdown of the gameplay:

  1. Place Your Bet: Choose whether you think the next card will be higher or lower than the current one. The options are simple: "Up" or "Down".
  2. Wait for the Reveal: Once you’ve made your selection, the dealer will reveal the next number. If it’s higher than the current number, you win your "Up" bet, and if it’s lower, you win your "Down" bet.
